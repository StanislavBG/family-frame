Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

Overview

Family Frame ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our application at https://family-frame.replit.app.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: When you sign in, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture from your authentication provider (Clerk).
  • Google Photos Data: When you connect your Google Photos account, we access your photo albums to display them on your family frame. We only access albums you explicitly select.
  • Location Data: If you choose to set your home location, we store the city and country to display weather information.
  • Calendar Events: You may add calendar events that are stored to display on your family frame.

Google API Services User Data Policy Compliance

Family Frame's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Scopes We Request

We request the following Google OAuth scopes:

  • photoslibrary.readonly - To read and display your photo albums on your family frame

Limited Use Disclosure

Family Frame:

  • Only uses your Google Photos data to display photos on your family frame display - the core functionality of the app
  • Does NOT store your photos on our servers - photos are fetched directly from Google Photos when displayed
  • Does NOT share your Google data with any third parties
  • Does NOT use your Google data for advertising or marketing purposes
  • Does NOT allow humans to read your data unless you provide affirmative consent, it's necessary for security/legal purposes, or the data is aggregated for internal operations
  • Allows you to disconnect Google Photos access at any time from Settings

How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to provide the Family Frame service:

  • Display your selected photo albums on your family frame
  • Show weather information for your configured location
  • Display calendar events you've created
  • Connect you with family members who also use Family Frame

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Firebase Realtime Database with encryption in transit and at rest. We use industry-standard security measures including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure authentication via Clerk
  • OAuth 2.0 for Google Photos integration
  • Access tokens stored securely and never exposed to the client

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties. Your data is only shared with connected family members within the app as you explicitly choose.

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, all associated data is permanently removed from our systems within 30 days.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Disconnect third-party services (Google Photos) at any time via Settings
  • Revoke Google Photos access via Google Account Permissions
  • Export your data upon request

Children's Privacy

Family Frame is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us at:
support@family-frame.replit.app
