How We Use Your Google Data

Family Frame integrates with Google Photos to display your cherished memories on your family frame display. Here's exactly what we do with your data:

  • Read-only access: We only request permission to view your Google Photos albums (photoslibrary.readonly scope)
  • Your choice: You select which specific albums to display - we never access albums you haven't chosen
  • No storage: Your photos are streamed directly from Google Photos and are NOT stored on our servers
  • No sharing: We never share your Google data with third parties
  • No advertising: Your data is never used for advertising purposes
  • Revocable: You can disconnect Google Photos at any time from your account settings

Our use of Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.
